Unofficial El Triunfo Menu

El Triunfo is a Mexican restaurant in Northfield, Minnesota. Call 507-664-3888 for take-out. This unofficial menu was transcribed from photographs taken (with permission) in the restaurant during 2016 April. Because El Triunfo does not maintain this web page, it is not responsible for any outdated or incorrect information here.
